PRIZE & AWARDS

Participation itself is worth applauding and for this, every student earns a participation certificate.

  1. Participation Certificate to all the participants
  2. Merit certificates to all the toppers and Phase 2 selected students.
  3. Awards to all the class toppers
  4. Awards to state toppers
  5. Excellence in education award
  6. Educator of the year award
  7. Best School awards
  8. Best Coordinator awards
  9. Medal of excellence to the toppers
  10. Gold, Silver & Bronze medals

    11. (all the medals are gold/silver/bronze color plated)

PRIZES FOR
PHASE 1

  1. All the participants will get certificate of participation.
  2. All the Phase 2 qualified students will get Medal and Certificate of Merit.

    3. (all the medals are gold/silver/bronze color plated)

CRITERIA FOR PHASE-II
SELECTION

  1. State Rank 1 to 10 will be qualified.
  2. Class toppers (of any particular subject) who have scored a minimum of 50% in the exam and a minimum of 5 students who have participated from that particular class in that particular subject only, will be qualified.
