My Pooch Face

My Pooch Face is an online art gallery with professional artists that create custom pet portraits from customer’s favorite pet photos. These portraits are hand drawn by a professional team of digital artists with a stylus & tablet or directly on to the monitor using sophisticated fine art software. Every detail is drawn with meticulous attention to accurately capture your pet. Their goal is to create a perfect work of art that you can cherish forever.

Challenges
  • A custom plugin to integrate with WordPress/ WooCommerce.
  • Choosing the right technology and agency partner.
  • Providing a smooth user experience.
  • Optimizing the website for different platforms.
  • Get a loyal and happy client-base.
Solutions
  • Designed a custom plugin, according to the client.
  • Optimized the website for desktop and mobile platforms.
  • Successful integration of the plugin without any deprivation.
Benefits

Gyrix Impact

  • 75% visible rise in the website’s traffic due to custom plugin & better UI-UX.

Choosing to work with Gyrix was one of the best decisions that we at My Pooch Face have made. The quality of work they provide, the effort they make for delivering the best has made us believe in having a long-term relationship with them.
