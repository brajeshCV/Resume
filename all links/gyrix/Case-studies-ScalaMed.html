ScalaMed

ScalaMed is the technology partner for the patients, doctors, pharmacists, and general practitioners where they can get access to their medical records, anytime, anywhere through the App. They focus on providing Rx management and fulfilment, as it is the perfect feature to offer to your patients, ensuring they come and utilize your application again and again. It’s all about creating new opportunities to interact with the patients.

Challenges
  • Providing personalized experience to the patients and make everything available on their fingertips.
  • Integrating technology with the old & traditional way of healthcare industry.
  • Providing easy access to the patients to retrieve & share their medical data.
  • Bringing down the doctors, patients, pharmacists, & care-givers to one common platform.
  • Creating a secured database system to keep all the data safe.
Solutions
  • Created a successful web-application for the general practitioners & pharmacies.
  • Also, a mobile application for the patients to easily fetch all their medical data.
  • Using blockchain technology, makes every information safe & secure that can be retrieved anytime.
  • Provided authorised data integration system.
Benefits
  • Patients didn’t face any issue while retrieving their data and choosing their doctors.
  • The happy customer base increased to a good number.
  • Trust & reliability of the patients increased with the implementation of the Blockchain technology.
  • Improved user experience.
  • Digital transformation of the medical industry.
  • Fetching the medical & health-related data became easy & faster.
Gyrix Impact
  • The proportion of site visitors grew by 55%.
  • Significant number of people started downloading & trusting the ScalaMed App.
  • The ratio of happy & satisfied users increased by 80%
